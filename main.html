Story

Coffee is a drink prepared from roasted coffee beans. Darkly colored, bitter, and slightly acidic, coffee has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. It is the most popular hot drink in the world.

Bicerin

Traditional hot drink native to Turin, Italy, made of espresso, drinking chocolate, and milk served layered in a small glass.

Consistency:

  1. White chocolate
  2. Dark chocolate
  3. Espresso
  4. Whipped cream

Mocha

Chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a caffè latte commonly served in a glass rather than a mug.

Consistency:

  1. Chocolate sprinkles
  2. Whipped cream
  3. Steamed milk
  4. Chocolate syrup
  5. Espresso

Brewers Cup

This is a championship where they compete in the skill of making specialty class filter coffee. Baristas prepare coffee, after which they are given 7 to 10 minutes to speak to the judges, during which they talk about the taste of the drink. There is an established order of description here.

Coffee aroma
The nature of the aroma is determined: refreshing, sweet, rich or spicy.

Taste
Since the taste of coffee is highly dependent on temperature, three descriptions are given:
  1. in a hot drink (70 °C)
  2. in a warm drink (40 °C)
  3. in cooled (25 °C)
Aftertaste
This is the duration of the taste sensation on the back of the palate that remains after a sip. The participant describes its duration and then gives a rating of its character: refreshing, sweet, rich.
Sourness
The participant characterizes it according to three parameters:
  1. Type
  2. Quality
  3. Intensity
Body
This is the name given to the tactile sensations experienced by the tongue. The body has:
  1. Weight or intensity (average, above average, or below average)
  2. Quality or tactility (enveloping, smooth, velvety)
Balance
If all the components listed complement each other, the coffee is balanced. For example, equal proportions of acidity and sweetness do not mean there is a balance.
